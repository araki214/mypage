What is 'Information retrival?'

We acquire infrmation by query retrival. However, this approach is uneffective when a retriever does not know about the topic of the retrieval target. Therefore, I think we should construct the system by which a retriver can acquire information of unfamiliar topics.
I propose 'information retrival based on metaphor.'
I think we can regard one sentence written about an unfamiliar topic as metaphor. Based on the idea, I suppose the situation that by giving one sentence, a retriever can get the sentences which share the gist.
